# v1.4.8 — Bug fixes

## What's been fixed?

- Removing a video from the queue no longer switches everyone to the wrong video — if you deleted something that was earlier in the list, the currently playing video would silently change. It won't anymore.
- When the currently playing video is removed from the queue, the next one now properly starts from the beginning instead of picking up from a random position.
- If your connection dropped right as you were joining a Discord session, the join button could get permanently stuck and stop responding. That's fixed.
- After a very long pause followed by jumping back to the start of something, players could end up frozen at the very end of the video instead of where they were supposed to be.
- Chat no longer janks when you rejoin a room — previously it would animate-scroll through the entire chat history at once, which looked awful.
- It's now harder to spam the chat (server now enforces its own limits, not just the app).
