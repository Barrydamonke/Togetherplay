# v1.4.7 — Discord improvements & sync fixes

## What's new?

- Discord rooms are now locked to your voice channel — only people in the same call can join, web users who somehow know the room details get turned away
- The sidebar shows your voice channel name instead of a useless PIN
- The sync indicator now actually tells you something — if you're drifting from the host it shows how far (e.g. "2.3s behind") with an amber dot, and it stays visible even when the controls fade out so you always know
- Pausing no longer occasionally skips the video forward
- Viewers now snap to the host's exact pause position instead of being allowed to drift up to 2 seconds either side — this stops small out-of-sync moments from quietly accumulating
- If you somehow drift more than 10 seconds from the host while playing, you'll automatically snap back
